# ZWISERFIT Constitution / ZWISERFIT 宪法

> **The Basic Law of ZWISERFIT Open Source Community Collaboration**
> **ZWISERFIT 开源社区协作基本法**

---

## Preamble

ZWISERFIT is not a capital-packaged narrative. It is a real-world physical store entrepreneur's journey of building an AI legion from zero, in a real store, with real sweat and real losses. This Constitution governs how we build, how we collaborate, and how we earn trust.

ZWISERFIT 不是资本包装的叙事。它是一个真实实体创业者，在真实门店，用真实汗水与真实亏损，从零训练AI军团的历程。本宪法约束我们如何建设、如何协作、如何赢得信任。

---

## Article I: Data Sovereignty Supreme / 数据主权至上

**EN:** Every data point in the ZWISERFIT ecosystem originates from a real human action in a real physical space. Data sovereignty belongs to the individual who generated it — the member who exercised, the coach who trained, the store that operated. No data may be fabricated, extrapolated without clear labeling, or presented as real when it is not. **Data without provenance is not data — it is deception.**

**中文：** ZWISERFIT 生态系统中的每一条数据，源于真实物理空间中真实人类的真实行为。数据主权归属数据生产者——锻炼的会员、指导的教练、运营的门店。任何数据不得编造、不得在无明确标注的情况下推算、不得将非真实数据冒充为真实数据呈报。**没有来源的数据不是数据——是欺骗。**

### Principle 1.1: Data Provenance Mandate
- Every dataset in this repository MUST include a `SOURCE.md` or equivalent provenance declaration
- Real-time data, test data, historical data, and simulated data MUST be clearly labeled
- Contributors MUST distinguish between what they measured versus what they inferred

### Principle 1.2: The "Brain Supplement" Zero-Tolerance Rule
- AI agents within ZWISERFIT are forbidden from generating summary reports that mix real and fabricated data
- Any data report MUST declare its data source tier: `[L1 - Measured]`, `[L2 - Test-derived]`, `[L3 - Historical Reference]`
- Generating unlabeled aggregate data reports (e.g., "Today's foot traffic: X people") when the data pipeline is not connected constitutes data fraud

## Article II: Physical Trust / 物理信任

**EN:** We rebuild trust from the physical world upward, not from blockchain narratives downward. The store is the root of trust. Every member interaction, every training session, every punch-card record — these are the atomic units of trust in our system. RWA (Real World Assets) means nothing without the "R" — Real.

**中文：** 我们自物理世界向上重构信任，而非从区块链叙事向下包装。门店是信任之根。每一次会员交互、每一次训练、每一次打卡——这些是系统信任的原子单元。RWA（真实世界资产）若无"真实"，则毫无意义。

### Principle 2.1: Store-First Architecture
- All technical decisions must serve the physical store operations first
- No feature may be deployed to the cloud that cannot function in a single physical store with limited connectivity
- The store operator's experience is the ultimate quality metric

### Principle 2.2: Offline-First Design
- Core systems must function without constant internet connectivity
- Data sync is eventual, not immediate — reliability over speed
- The AI store manager (Momo) must be capable of autonomous operation during network outages

---

## Article III: Open Source True Transparency / 开源真透明

**EN:** We do not open-source for marketing. We open-source because building trust infrastructure requires radical transparency. Our code, our architecture, our operational data, our failures, our pivots — all committed to the public record. **You cannot audit what you cannot see.**

**中文：** 我们开源不为营销。我们开源是因为构建信任基础设施需要极致透明。我们的代码、架构、运营数据、失败教训、战略转向——全部提交至公开记录。**无法审视的，便无法信任。**

### Principle 3.1: Commit Reality
- Operational data commits to GitHub daily, not selectively
- Failures and incidents are documented with the same rigor as successes
- All significant architectural decisions are recorded as GitHub Issues with technical context

### Principle 3.2: Public Roadmap
- The project roadmap lives in GitHub Issues and Projects, visible to all
- Feature prioritization rationale is documented
- Breaking changes are announced with migration guides

---

## Article IV: AI Collaboration, Not Human Replacement / AI协同非人力替代

**EN:** Our AI agents are collaborators, not replacements. They handle repetitive execution so humans can focus on strategy, creativity, and relationship-building. The store owner is not replaced — they are elevated from operator to strategist. The coach is not replaced — they are freed from paperwork to focus on members.

**中文：** 我们的AI代理是协作者，非替代者。它们处理重复执行，使人类得以聚焦战略、创造与关系建设。店主不被替代——他们从执行者跃升为战略家。教练不被替代——他们从文书工作中解放，专注于会员。

### Principle 4.1: Human-in-the-Loop
- AI agents operate within clearly defined authority boundaries
- Strategic decisions (pricing, partnerships, major changes) require human approval
- Any AI agent can be overridden by its human supervisor

### Principle 4.2: AI Accountability
- Every AI agent has a documented capability boundary
- AI failures must be reported within 3 minutes to the human chain of command
- "Silent failure" (hiding or glossing over errors) is a constitutional violation

---

## Article V: Community as Co-Builders / 社区即共建者

**EN:** ZWISERFIT is not "my project" — it is infrastructure for the physical fitness industry. Contributors are not "helping me" — they are building infrastructure they will use. Every pull request, every bug report, every feature suggestion shapes the protocol that could govern their own store.

**中文：** ZWISERFIT不是"我的项目"——它是实体健身行业的基础设施。贡献者不是在"帮我"——他们在建设自己将使用的基础设施。每一个Pull Request、每一个Bug报告、每一个功能建议，都在塑造可能管理他们自己门店的协议。

### Principle 5.1: Contributor Sovereignty
- Contributors retain full rights to their contributions (Apache 2.0)
- The project has no single "Benevolent Dictator" — governance evolves with the community
- Major decisions require public RFC (Request for Comments) Issues

### Principle 5.2: Lower the Bar for Contribution
- `good first issue` labels are a commitment, not decoration
- Every Issue includes reproduction steps and technical context sufficient for new contributors
- Documentation quality is a first-class concern, not an afterthought

---

## Article VI: Amendment Process / 修订程序

**EN:** This Constitution can be amended by:
1. Opening a Constitutional Amendment Issue in the ZWISERFIT/ZWISERFIT repository
2. Public discussion period of minimum 7 days
3. Approval by at least 2 core maintainers and no sustained objections from store operators

**中文：** 本宪法可通过以下程序修订：
1. 在 ZWISERFIT/ZWISERFIT 仓库中开设宪法修正Issue
2. 至少7天公开讨论期
3. 至少2位核心维护者批准，且无门店运营者的持续反对
